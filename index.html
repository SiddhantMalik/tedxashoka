Ashoka University

Ashoka University is a philanthropic initiative with a strong emphasis on cross-disciplinary engagement. The university offers programs in a wide range of disciplines, including the humanities, social sciences, natural sciences, and computer science. Its curriculum is based on a liberal arts model that emphasizes broad-based learning and critical thinking.

The university has a diverse student body, with students from across India and around the world. The faculty members are acclaimed academics and researchers in their respective fields, with many professors coming from highly-ranked universities.

TEDxAshokaUniversity

TEDxAshokaUniversity is a platform through which we aim to unleash new ideas, inspire and inform. The objective is to promote powerful storytelling, cross-disciplinary interactions, and untapped talents who have the aptitude to create and inspire change. Established in 2015, TEDxAshokaUniversity embodies the passion and spirit of the university.
